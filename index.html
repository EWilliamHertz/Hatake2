Our Story

Founded in 2025

Hatake TCG was founded by Ernst-William Hertz and Mark Jensen, passionate TCG enthusiasts committed to creating premium, sustainable accessories. Our journey began in Falköping, Sweden, with a vision to revolutionize TCG gear while supporting Nordic industry.

Our Mission

We combine Swedish design with ethical manufacturing, currently partnering with a trusted Chinese supplier while working toward full Nordic production. Every product undergoes rigorous testing - our sleeves last 20% longer than competitors!

Why Choose Hatake?

  • Premium quality matching Dragon Shield/KMC
  • Unique Nordic-designed products
  • Ethical manufacturing practices
  • Supporting Swedish industry
  • Competitive retailer margins
